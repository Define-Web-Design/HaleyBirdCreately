# Directive: Keep the App Running in Replit's Development Environment

## Objective
Ensure the app remains active and accessible within the Replit development environment without requiring deployment or session interruptions. Maintain smooth workflows and structural integrity for testing while allowing auto-login functionality for streamlined access.

---

## Implementation Steps

### 1. **Ensure Continuous App Running**
- Utilize Replit's built-in settings to keep the app running:
  - Enable the **"Always On"** feature to prevent the app from timing out or shutting down due to inactivity.
  - If "Always On" is not available, use Replit's `console` or keep the environment active with regular pings from an internal function or external service (e.g., UptimeRobot).

### 2. **Enable Auto-Login for Development**
- Use a `devMode=true` flag in the app configuration to bypass login for testing purposes:
  - Skip authentication checks when `devMode=true` is active.
  - Allow access to protected routes and features in the development environment.

### 3. **Session Persistence**
- Implement session persistence during development:
  - Store session tokens temporarily in **localStorage** or **cookies**.
  - Automatically renew session tokens during testing without requiring re-login.

### 4. **Debug the "App Not Running" Issue**
- Check if the app's initialization or routing logic is causing the "App not running" message.
- Ensure all required dependencies are installed and properly initialized in the Replit environment.
- Test the app's startup process in the Replit console to confirm proper execution.

---

## Testing Guidelines
1. **Continuous Access Verification**:
   - Confirm the app remains running and accessible in Replit's development environment.
   - Test auto-login functionality across all features without requiring manual login.

2. **Session Stability**:
   - Verify that session tokens persist across updates during testing.
   - Test session renewal to ensure uninterrupted access during extended testing sessions.

3. **Integration with Previous Changes**:
   - Ensure the auto-login mechanism and session persistence do not interfere with other app features or workflows.
   - Validate compatibility with Phase 1 security features, such as CSRF protection and token expiry.

---

## Notes
- All changes should be isolated to the development environment and must not affect the production configuration.
- When transitioning to production, remove the `devMode` flag and re-enable full authentication workflows.

This directive ensures the app remains accessible and functional in Replit's development environment, streamlining your testing process while preserving the app's structural integrity for future deployment.
