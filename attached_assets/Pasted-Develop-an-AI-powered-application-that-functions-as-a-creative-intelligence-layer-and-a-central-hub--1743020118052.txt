Develop an AI-powered application that functions as a creative intelligence layer and a central hub for organizing, optimizing, and enhancing social media content, seamlessly integrating into the Apple Photos ecosystem.

Core Functionality:
- Integrated Ecosystem: Sync with Apple Photos to leverage existing themes and extend them with AI-powered enhancements. Generate dynamic themes based on audience analytics, personal preferences, and growth patterns. Serve as an overarching intelligence layer, enriching the native Photos experience with data-informed creative insights.

Machine Learning and Performance Analysis:
- Use machine learning algorithms to analyze content performance metrics, predict engagement trends, and adjust recommendations in real time. Provide performance forecasting for optimized post scheduling and engagement improvements.

Content Optimization Tools:
- Refinement Options: Offer Maintain (preserve content), Tweak (adjust to capture emerging interests), and Enhance (optimize specific elements).  
- Auto-Curation Mode: Dynamically group photos, videos, and stories into curated posts based on trending aesthetics, audience insights, and performance patterns.  
- Creative Prompts and Challenges: Include guided prompts, such as “Minimalism with soft tones,” to spark creativity.

Enhanced Visualization and Planning:
- Predictive Mood Boards and Storyboarding: Visualize post aesthetics and plan content flow with suggested captions, hashtags, and layout options.  

Cross-Platform and Global Optimization:
- Customize and optimize content for platforms like Instagram and TikTok, ensuring cohesive visual identity. Include cultural context filters to help tailor content for global audiences.

Added Functionalities:
- Visual Trend Analysis: Provide AI-driven insights into emerging trends and audience preferences.  
- Gamification: Reward creative exploration with badges, milestones, and incentives like “Trendsetter” or “Master Curator.”  
- AI Voiceover and Captioning: Offer automated caption suggestions and voiceovers tailored to thematic undertones and trending styles.  
- Sustainability Tracker: Identify evergreen content for optimal reposting or refreshing.  
- Interactive Tutorials and Creative Collaborations: Guide users through features with tutorials and recommend collaborations by analyzing shared interests.  
- Content Archive Vault: Maintain a vault for unused or older content, suggesting remix ideas to create new posts.  
- Community Polling and Live Analytics: Enable instant audience feedback via polls and provide real-time recommendations during peak engagement periods.

Longevity, Accessibility, and Adaptability:
- Ensure adaptability to emerging platforms, audience trends, and technological advancements.  
- Embed accessibility features such as alternative text generation, audio descriptions, and interface adjustments for inclusivity.  

Regulatory and User Experience Considerations:
- Ensure compliance with internet laws and SEO best practices.  
- Design a sleek, modern, and intuitive UX/UI with dynamic ambient gradients inspired by natural lighting shifts.  
- Incorporate user feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement, keeping design and functionality aligned with user needs.

Style Guide:
- Colors: Primary #F2994A (warm orange), Secondary #FF9DAE (soft magenta), Background #FAFAFA (cloud white for light mode) and #121212 (charcoal for dark mode), Accent #6FCF97 (fresh green), Text #333333 (slate gray).  
- Design: Inter and SF Pro Display fonts, card-based content grid with 24px spacing, floating action buttons, responsive design supporting intuitive drag-and-drop interfaces with light/dark mode compatibility.  

Overall Vision:
Create a visually striking, data-driven platform that empowers social media creators by blending advanced analytics, creative freedom, and user-centric design. This app will revolutionize content creation while remaining inclusive and globally appealing.
